Why is my website called Saint Bautista's?

Well, for starters, my middle name is "Santos", which is Spanish for "Saint". So, Saint Bautista's it became. Fun fact: San Juan Bautista is the Spanish-language name of the Saint John the Baptist. Here's another fun fact: "Keoni", my second first name, means "John" in Hawaiian, which is my dad's name. So, Christian Keoni Santos Bautista it is.

Yeah, that's bound to give you a headache.
